Honey

Just honey, honey

Ingredients

  • 1 beehive
  • 1-2 portions of courage

Tools

  • One box/glass/small container
  • Gloves
  • Beekeeper suit if available
  • Bear spray (depends on your location)

Steps

  1. Take on Beekeeper suite, gloves and get bear spray, as well as the small container ready.

  2. Go to the beehive.

  3. Get your honey by using your courage now.

  4. Run.

  5. Pray to the old gods and the new, that the bees give up quicker than you.

  6. In case you still live, enjoy your honey
